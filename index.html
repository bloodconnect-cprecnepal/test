Understanding Blood Donation

One donation can save up to three lives. Here is what you need to know.

Blood Types

There are 8 main blood types. O- is the universal donor, while AB+ is the universal recipient.

A+ A- B+ O+ O- AB+

Eligibility

Most people can donate. You just need to be healthy, over 18 years old, and weigh at least 50kg.

Why Donate?

Blood cannot be manufactured. It can only come from generous donors like you. Every 2 seconds, someone needs blood.
